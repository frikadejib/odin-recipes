Pork shish kebab

A great way to marinate pork so that it remains juicy even after simmering on coals.

  • Pork - 1.5 kg
  • Small onions - 10-12 pcs.
  • Large tomato - 1 pc.
  • Lemon - 0.5 pcs.
  • Salt- 1 h.
  • Ground pepper - 1 tsp.
  1. Prepare ingredients for pork barbecue. Cut the onion into rings, cut the tomatoes into slices.
  2. Rinse and dry the meat. Cut into small pieces, the size of a matchbox.
  3. Place a layer of meat on the bottom of the container, salt and pepper on top.
  4. Place onion rings over pork.
  5. Place tomato slices on the onion. Sprinkle with lemon.
  6. Repeat the procedure until all the meat has been placed. Cover and marinate pork with onions and tomatoes in the refrigerator for at least 8 hours. You can shake the container periodically so that the juices are evenly distributed and the meat is better marinated.
  7. Fry pork shish kebab on the grill.
  8. Pork shish kebab is ready. Pour a glass of wine (or whatever you want) and enjoy excellent meat in good company. Bon appetit!
